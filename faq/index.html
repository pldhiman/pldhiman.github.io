FAQs

Do OnePrep videos work on all devices?

To watch OnePrep videos on iOS, you need iOS version 10 and above, and for Android, you need version 6 and above.
However, there are few devices which are not supported because of few security reasons.

  • Lenovo K3 Note
  • Lenovo K5 Note
  • Asus ZenFone
  • Xiaomi Redmi 4a
  • Other Redmi models
  • Lava phones

The above list is also device specific which means some phones of the above models can support OnePrep content, while others may have issues. As much as we want OnePrep to benefit all the students, but if you have any of the above mentioned devices, we will advice you to play one of the free videos in OnePrep app on your device before going for a Premium plan.

What will happen to my plan if I change my phone or device or number?

Your plan will be associated with the email id you use while enrolling. If you change your device, all your data, bookmarks, tests and usage is safe online. However, the downloaded videos will be lost in the process of device change. Just login to the other device and you will be good to go from where you left.

How can I ask Doubts?

There will be a section in the app to ask doubts from specific OnePrep faculty, wherein you can ask unlimited doubts from the subject experts.

How many times can I watch the videos?

You can watch the videos unlimited times during the subscription of your pack.

What is the refund policy?

All purchases will be final and no refund request will be entertained under any circumstances.

Will the OnePrep videos be available on Laptop?

Due to security concerns, we wouldn’t be able to provide laptop access to the videos.
